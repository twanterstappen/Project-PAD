Info

Are you ready to put your hacking skills to the test? Look no further! CyberCTF is the premier online platform designed exclusively for cybersecurity enthusiasts like you. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, CyberCTF offers an immersive and challenging experience for all levels of expertise.

What is CyberCTF?

CyberCTF is a cutting-edge website created as part of a school project in the field of Cyber Security. Our aim is to provide a safe and educational environment where users can enhance their cybersecurity knowledge and skills through Capture the Flag (CTF) challenges. Each CTF is carefully designed to simulate real-world scenarios, focusing primarily on the Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP) Top 10 vulnerabilities.

How Does CyberCTF Work?

Signing up on CyberCTF is simple and free! Once you've created your account, you'll gain access to a wide range of CTF challenges spanning various categories. Each challenge is meticulously crafted to test your understanding of security concepts and techniques, encouraging you to think like a hacker and find vulnerabilities in vulnerable applications.
