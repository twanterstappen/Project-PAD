Welcome to our SQL injection CTF! The objective of this game is to use your knowledge of SQL injection attacks to exploit vulnerabilities in our web application and retrieve sensitive data.

Playing a CTF game in the world of hacking is the same as a real life capture the flag game. You capture the flag from your enemy team and bring it back to your side. The same principle goes here. You capture a flag, in this case it wil be a random couple of characters. You will have to try to find it somewhere in the code that is part of our website. After finding a flag you are able to submit this flag, in the case that it is incorrect you will have to try again. If this is not the case and you have successfully found the flag and submitted it this will be registered on the website. By registering on our website you become a CyberCTF member and will be able to keep track of the CTF challenges that you have already completed


Here's how to play:

  1. First, navigate to our web application at our page. You'll be presented with a login form that requires a username and password.

  2. Your goal is to use SQL injection techniques to bypass the login form and access the sensitive data stored on our server.

  3. To do this, you'll need to enter specially crafted SQL statements into the username and/or password fields. These statements will exploit vulnerabilities in the web application's code and allow you to retrieve data that you shouldn't have access to.

  4. As you progress through the levels, the difficulty will increase and the vulnerabilities will become more complex. Some levels may require a combination of techniques to bypass the login form.
  5. If you get stuck or need a hint, feel free to ask the organizers for assistance. We're here to help you learn and have fun.

  6. Remember to be ethical and responsible with your skills. SQL injection attacks can be used for malicious purposes, and we strongly discourage any such activity. Our goal is to educate and raise awareness about these vulnerabilities, not to encourage harmful behavior.

Good luck and have fun!
